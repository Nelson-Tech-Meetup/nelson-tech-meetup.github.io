Cozy up with your favourite tea and take in 3 engaging talks on Artificial Intelligence by local tech experts!

## Ralph Wiggum Loops
**Matt Wynne**

What does Ralph Wiggum have to do with software engineering? Matt will share his experiences playing with Ralph loops, a technique for letting robots like Claude code run unattended. He'll talk about other multi-agent workflows and tools including his own "yak mapping" tool.

## The State of the Art
**Ola Rogula**

Artificial Intelligence continues to be a controversial topic in the artistic fields. Ola will give an update of the state of the tool among industries and artistic fields, and what we can learn from looking at the integration of similar yet different tools in the past.
