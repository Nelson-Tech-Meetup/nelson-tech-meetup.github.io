## About Us

We are a community of technology professionals, digital workers, and knowledge enthusiasts in the beautiful Kootenay region of British Columbia. Our meetups bring together:

- Software developers and engineers
- Digital marketers and content creators
- Remote workers and digital nomads
- Tech entrepreneurs and startup founders
- Students and lifelong learners

Join us for engaging talks, hands-on workshops, and relaxed networking in Nelson BC.
